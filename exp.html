Olympic Computer Camp
During my fourth year in high school, I had the opportunity to participate in the Olympic Computer Camp 1. However, I encountered a setback when I did not succeed in the entrance examination for Camp 2. In my fifth year, I once again applied for the Olympic Computer Camp 1 and successfully passed the examination to qualify for Camp 2. Unfortunately, my participation in Camp 2 was impeded by the unforeseen circumstances of the COVID-19 pandemic.
